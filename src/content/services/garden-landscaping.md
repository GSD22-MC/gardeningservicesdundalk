## Professional Garden Landscaping in Dundalk

Looking to completely transform your outdoor space? Our garden landscaping service covers everything from initial design to the finished product.

### What We Offer

- **Garden Design** — We work with you to plan the perfect layout for your space
- **Hard Landscaping** — Patios, paths, retaining walls, raised beds, and steps
- **Soft Landscaping** — Planting, turfing, borders, and garden beds
- **Water Features** — Ponds, fountains, and drainage solutions
- **Garden Lighting** — Outdoor lighting to enjoy your garden after dark
