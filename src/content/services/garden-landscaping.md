## Professional Garden Landscaping in Dundalk & County Louth

Looking to completely transform your outdoor space? Our garden landscaping service covers everything from initial design through to the finished product. Pete and Seamus have been designing and building gardens across Dundalk and County Louth for over 35 years — and every project is completed with the same attention to detail, whether it's a small courtyard or a full rear garden transformation.

### What We Offer

- **Garden Design** — We work with you to plan the perfect layout for your space, style, and budget
- **Hard Landscaping** — Patios, paths, retaining walls, raised beds, steps, and edging
- **Soft Landscaping** — Turfing, planting schemes, borders, shrub beds, and mature planting
- **Water Features** — Ponds, fountains, and drainage solutions
- **Garden Lighting** — Outdoor lighting to enjoy your garden after dark
- **Outdoor Living** — Pergolas, seating areas, fire pits, and entertaining spaces

You'll get a detailed written quote with no obligation — and no hidden costs. Once approved, we handle everything from groundwork to planting, and always leave your property spotless.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does garden landscaping cost in Dundalk?

Every garden is different, so costs vary depending on the size of the space, the materials used, and the complexity of the design. We offer free, no-obligation quotes so you'll know the exact cost before any work begins. As a guide, small courtyard projects can start from around €2,000, while full rear garden transformations typically range from €5,000 to €15,000+.

### How long does a garden landscaping project take?

Most garden landscaping projects take between 1 and 3 weeks, depending on the scope of work. Smaller projects like a patio and planting scheme can be completed in a few days. Larger transformations involving groundwork, drainage, paving, and planting may take 2-3 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start.

### Do you provide a garden design before starting?

Yes. We discuss your ideas during a free site visit, then provide a detailed plan and written quote. For larger projects, we can provide a sketch layout showing the proposed design. We want you to be completely happy with the plan before any work begins.

### What areas near Dundalk do you cover for landscaping?

We cover all of Dundalk and County Louth, including Blackrock, Carlingford, Ardee, Castlebellingham, Annagassan, Omeath, Louth Village, and all surrounding areas. If you're within County Louth, we can help.

### Can you landscape a new-build garden?

Absolutely. New-build gardens are one of our specialities. We handle everything from clearing builder's rubble and levelling the ground, to laying patios, erecting fencing, turfing, and planting. We can turn a bare site into a finished garden ready to enjoy.

### Are you insured for garden landscaping work?

Yes, we are fully insured for all garden landscaping work. This covers both public liability and any work carried out on your property. You can have complete peace of mind that your home and garden are protected.
