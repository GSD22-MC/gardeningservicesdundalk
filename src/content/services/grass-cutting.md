## Grass Cutting & Lawn Care in Dundalk

A well-maintained lawn is the foundation of any great garden. Whether you need a regular cut or a full lawn restoration, Pete and Seamus have the experience and equipment to keep your grass looking its best all year round.

### Our Lawn Services

- **Regular Grass Cutting** — Weekly, fortnightly, or one-off cuts tailored to your schedule
- **Lawn Edging** — Clean, defined edges along paths, borders, and flower beds
- **Scarifying** — Removal of thatch and moss to promote healthy growth
- **Top-Dressing & Overseeding** — Levelling out uneven areas and thickening up tired lawns
- **Lawn Treatments** — Seasonal feeds, weed control, and moss treatments

### Serving Dundalk & County Louth

With over 35 years of experience, we know exactly what local lawns need to thrive in the Irish climate. From small front gardens to large open spaces, no lawn is too big or too small.
