## Professional Hedge Trimming in Dundalk

Overgrown or untidy hedges can let down the appearance of any property. Pete and Seamus provide expert hedge trimming and shaping to keep your boundaries neat, healthy, and well-maintained throughout the year.

### What We Offer

- **Hedge Cutting & Shaping** — Regular trimming to maintain size and shape
- **Overgrown Hedge Restoration** — Bringing neglected hedges back under control
- **Hedge Removal** — Full removal of unwanted hedges, including root extraction
- **Conifer & Laurel Specialists** — We handle all common hedge types including leylandii, privet, beech, and laurel
- **Waste Removal** — All cuttings cleared and disposed of responsibly

### Why Choose Pete & Seamus?

With 35+ years of hands-on experience across Dundalk and County Louth, we have the right tools and know-how for hedges of every size. Whether it is a small front garden hedge or a large boundary line, we get the job done safely and efficiently.
