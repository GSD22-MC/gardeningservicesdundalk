## Fencing Installation in Dundalk & County Louth

Secure your property and enhance your garden with quality fencing from Pete and Seamus. From traditional panel fencing to custom gates, we install fencing that looks great and lasts for years. With 35+ years of hands-on experience, we serve homeowners across Dundalk and all of County Louth.

### Our Fencing Services

- **Panel Fencing** — Traditional closeboard, lap panel, and hit-and-miss fencing
- **Post & Rail Fencing** — Rustic and agricultural-style fencing for larger properties
- **Picket Fencing** — Classic front garden boundary fencing
- **Gate Installation** — Pedestrian and driveway gates to match your fencing
- **Storm Damage Repairs** — Fast repair or replacement of damaged panels and posts
- **Concrete Posts & Gravel Boards** — Long-lasting foundations for fencing that stays solid

### Built to Withstand the Irish Weather

Every fence we build is constructed with proper foundations, quality fixings, and treated timber to withstand the wet and windy Irish conditions. We use concrete posts and gravel boards where appropriate to prevent rot and ensure your fence stands strong for years. All work comes with a free, no-obligation quote, and we handle everything from old fence removal to installation to cleanup.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does fencing cost in Dundalk?

Fencing costs depend on the length of the run, the type of fencing, and whether old fencing needs to be removed. We provide free quotes with a full breakdown of costs so there are no surprises. Standard panel fencing with concrete posts typically starts from around €50-€70 per metre, depending on specifications.

### How long does fencing installation take?

Most garden fencing jobs are completed in 1-2 days, depending on the length of the run and the type of fencing. We remove old fencing, install new concrete posts and gravel boards where appropriate, and leave the site tidy. Larger boundary fencing projects may take 2-3 days.

### Do you remove old fencing?

Yes. We remove and dispose of all old fencing and posts as part of the job. This is included in our quote — there are no hidden charges.

### Can you repair storm-damaged fencing?

Yes. We offer fast repair or replacement of storm-damaged panels, posts, and gates. We'll assess the damage and recommend whether a repair or full replacement is the best option.

### What areas do you cover for fencing?

We cover Dundalk and all of County Louth, including Blackrock, Carlingford, Ardee, Castlebellingham, Omeath, Lordship, Louth Village, and all surrounding areas.
