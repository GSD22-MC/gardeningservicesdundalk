## Gardening Services in Knockbridge

Gardening Services Dundalk is proud to serve the community of Knockbridge, a village steeped in local history and situated southwest of Dundalk along the Fane river valley. Pete and Seamus offer a comprehensive range of gardening services to residents here, including landscaping, grass cutting, hedge trimming, decking, paving, and complete garden clearance. The mix of older country homes and newer builds in the Knockbridge area means every garden is different, and our father and son team brings the experience needed to handle them all.
