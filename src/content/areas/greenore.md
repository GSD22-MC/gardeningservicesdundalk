## Gardening Services in Greenore

Gardening Services Dundalk is happy to serve the quiet coastal village of Greenore on the Cooley Peninsula. Pete and Seamus offer landscaping, grass cutting, hedge trimming, decking, paving, and complete garden clearance to homeowners throughout the area. Whether you are a permanent resident or maintain a holiday home in Greenore, our father and son team will ensure your garden looks its best in every season. Ring us for a free quote.
