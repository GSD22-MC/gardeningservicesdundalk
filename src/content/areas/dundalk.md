## Gardening Services in Dundalk

As the home base of Pete and Seamus, Dundalk is where Gardening Services Dundalk began and where we carry out the majority of our work. We know the town inside out, from the estates of Muirhevnamor and Bay Estate to the mature gardens of the Dublin Road and beyond. Our full range of services covers landscaping, grass cutting, hedge trimming, decking installation, paving, and garden clearance. As a father and son team rooted in the local community, we take pride in keeping Dundalk's gardens in top shape all year round. Contact us for a free quote.
