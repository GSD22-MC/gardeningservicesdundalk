## Gardening Services in Inniskeen

Gardening Services Dundalk is pleased to cover the village of Inniskeen, famous as the birthplace of poet Patrick Kavanagh and set in the rolling countryside along the Louth-Monaghan border. Pete and Seamus bring their professional gardening skills to this peaceful rural community, offering landscaping, grass cutting, hedge trimming, decking, paving, and garden clearance. Whether your property sits in the village itself or along the surrounding country roads, we provide dependable service with a personal touch. Reach out to our father and son team for a free quote on any garden project, large or small.
